## What's changed?

The TestFlight beta programme has served its purpose: your feedback made CallPlan better. The app is now stable, complete, and available in the official App Store.

**TestFlight is no longer supported.** Please download the app directly from the App Store to receive automatic updates.

---

## Your beta benefits stay

As a beta tester, you were with CallPlan from the beginning. All premium features you unlocked during the beta period remain active – no need to pay again.

Questions? [support@quick-id.com](mailto:support@quick-id.com)

---

## What's new in the official version?

- ☁️ Cloud sync across all devices
- 📊 Call Insights & statistics
- 🔗 CRM integrations (HubSpot, Salesforce, Odoo, Zoho)
- ⚡ 40% faster app launch
- 🎙️ Improved Siri and CarPlay integration
