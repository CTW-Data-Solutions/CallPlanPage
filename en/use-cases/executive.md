## Most executives recognise this

<div class="persona-scenario">
<p>You have 200 important contacts. Investors who need an update. Partners you haven't spoken to in too long. A key client whose contract expires in 3 months. A candidate waiting on your feedback.</p>
<p>At some point, "I'll call next week" has real consequences: a deal goes to a competitor because you weren't present. An investor is surprised by bad quarterly results – because you didn't call ahead. A top performer signs elsewhere.</p>
<p><strong>Leadership rarely fails through lack of competence. It fails through lack of consistency.</strong></p>
</div>
