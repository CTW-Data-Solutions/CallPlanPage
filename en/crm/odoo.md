## How the connection works

<div class="crm-workflow partner--odoo">
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">1</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Connect your Odoo instance</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">In CallPlan go to Settings → Integrations → Sign in with Odoo. Supports Odoo Online (odoo.com) and self-hosted instances.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">2</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Link a call to an Odoo contact or lead</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">When creating a call, select the matching Odoo record. CallPlan shows you the existing conversation history right away.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">3</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Make the call – take notes hands-free</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">Dictate via Siri or CarPlay during the call. CallPlan stores everything in a structured way – no typing, no forgetting.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">4</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Automatic Odoo entry</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">After the call, the note appears automatically as an activity on the Odoo record. Follow-up tasks are created instantly. Zero manual effort.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
