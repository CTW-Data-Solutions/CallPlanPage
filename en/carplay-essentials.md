## The optimal CarPlay setup

For **frequent drivers and business users** we recommend this setup:

1. **Navigation:** Waze (real-time traffic) or Google Maps (best POIs)
2. **Call planning & notes:** CallPlan ← the decisive difference
3. **Audio:** Spotify (everything in one app) or Pocket Casts (podcasts only)
4. **Communication:** WhatsApp (or built-in Phone app)

> **Tip:** Keep your CarPlay display clean. More than 4–6 apps create confusion while driving. Quality over quantity.
