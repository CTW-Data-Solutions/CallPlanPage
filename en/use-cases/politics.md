## The reality of political work

<div class="persona-scenario">
<p>You're a candidate. Or an elected official. Or a campaign manager. Your day: appointments, press enquiries, committee meetings, local party work. In between: drives from A to B – 30, 40, sometimes 90 minutes.</p>
<p>In that time you could call 5 donors, update 3 local chairs, personally respond to 2 constituent concerns. Instead the radio plays, or you're mentally at the next appointment.</p>
<p><strong>Political relationship work doesn't fail through lack of will – it fails through lack of system.</strong> CallPlan is the system.</p>
</div>
