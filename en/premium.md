## Premium Subscription

The **Premium** subscription gives you full access to all features and content in CallPlan.

Your subscription will automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Payment will be charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase. You can manage and cancel your subscription at any time in your App Store account settings.

---

## Premium Features

- **Cloud Sync** – all your devices always up to date
- **Call Insights & Statistics** – call volume, duration and outcomes at a glance
- **CRM Integrations** – automatic call logging in HubSpot and Salesforce
- **Unlimited Calls** – no restrictions on the number of planned calls
- **Priority Support** – direct line to the developer
