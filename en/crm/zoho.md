## How the connection works

<div class="crm-workflow partner--zoho">
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">1</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Connect Zoho CRM</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">In CallPlan go to Settings → Integrations → Sign in with Zoho. Supports all Zoho CRM editions (Free, Standard, Professional, Enterprise).</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">2</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Link a call to a Zoho record</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">When creating a call, select the matching Zoho contact, lead or deal. Recent activities are displayed right away.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">3</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Make the call – take notes hands-free</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">Dictate via Siri or CarPlay during or right after the conversation. No typing required.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">4</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Automatic Zoho entry</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">Note and follow-up task appear automatically on the right Zoho record. Zero manual effort, complete conversation history.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
