## Why CallPlan + CRM?

CRM systems are your company's memory. But they don't help you **take notes during a call** – let alone hands-free while driving.

CallPlan closes exactly this gap: you plan, take notes, and follow up – CallPlan makes sure everything lands in the CRM.

## What CallPlan adds to your CRM

Your CRM manages contacts. CallPlan brings them to life.

<ul class="crm-feature-list partner--overview">
  <li class="crm-feature-item">
    <span class="crm-feature-icon">📞</span>
    <span><strong>Call planning with CRM context</strong> – open your next customer appointment with all relevant contact data from your CRM.</span>
  </li>
  <li class="crm-feature-item">
    <span class="crm-feature-icon">🎙️</span>
    <span><strong>Hands-free notes via Siri &amp; CarPlay</strong> – dictate call outcomes while you're still driving. No typing, no forgetting.</span>
  </li>
  <li class="crm-feature-item">
    <span class="crm-feature-icon">📤</span>

## Who benefits most?

| Role | Value |
|---|---|
| **Sales Rep / AE** | No more manual call logging – every customer call is instantly in the CRM |
| **Account Manager** | Complete conversation history with zero desk follow-up |
| **Sales Manager** | Cleaner CRM data, better reports, more coaching leverage |
| **Field Sales** | Take notes in the car on the way to the next appointment |
| **SDR / BDR** | Create follow-up tasks directly from the call |
