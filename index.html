About CallPlan

CallPlan is a smart scheduling and communication tool designed to streamline your workflow.

With CallPlan, you can easily manage planned calls, track important conversations, and integrate with your favorite productivity tools.

Features:

  • ✔ Easy call scheduling
  • ✔ Automated reminders
  • ✔ Call categorization
  • ✔ Cloud synchronization
  • ✔ Integration with productivity tools
