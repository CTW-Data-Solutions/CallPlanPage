## How the connection works

<div class="crm-workflow partner--hubspot">
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">1</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Connect your HubSpot account</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">In CallPlan go to Settings → Integrations → Sign in with HubSpot. The OAuth connection is set up in seconds.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">2</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Link a call to a HubSpot contact</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">When creating a call, select the matching HubSpot contact or deal. CallPlan shows you all relevant info right away.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">3</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Make the call – take notes hands-free</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">During the call, dictate via Siri or CarPlay. CallPlan stores everything in a structured way.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">4</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Automatic HubSpot entry</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">After the call, the note appears automatically as an activity on the HubSpot contact. Follow-up tasks are created. Zero manual effort.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
