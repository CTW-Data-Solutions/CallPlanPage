## What is AI Call Preparation?

CallPlan AI-powered preparation analyzes your past conversations with a contact, pulls CRM data, and creates a personalized conversation guide – so you go into every call fully prepared.

### 🚀 CallPlan AI prepares:

- **Customer history summarized** – Past calls, conversation flow at a glance
- **Personalized agenda** – What should you cover with this customer? AI suggests.
- **Open questions & follow-ups** – What was unresolved? Which promises need keeping?
- **Customer preferences & specifics** – What does the contact like? What to watch out for?
- **CRM data up-to-date** – Latest info from HubSpot, Salesforce, or Odoo
- **Conversation guide** – Proposal for optimal call structure
- **Alternative approaches** – "If X, then try Y" recommendations based on history
