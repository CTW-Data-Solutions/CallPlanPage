## How the connection works

<div class="crm-workflow partner--salesforce">
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">1</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Connect your Salesforce org</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">In CallPlan go to Settings → Integrations → Sign in with Salesforce. Supports both Sandbox and Production orgs.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">2</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Link a call to a Salesforce record</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">When creating a call, select the matching lead, contact or opportunity. CallPlan shows recent activities from Salesforce right away.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">3</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Make the call – take notes hands-free</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">Dictate via Siri or CarPlay during or right after the conversation. CallPlan stores everything structured – no typing required.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="crm-step">
    <div class="crm-step-num">4</div>
    <div class="crm-step-body">
      <p class="crm-step-title">Automatic Salesforce entry</p>
      <p class="crm-step-desc">Note, call duration and follow-up task appear automatically on the right Salesforce record. No manual data entry.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
