## What is AI Call Summarization?

CallPlan AI automatically transcribes your business calls and creates structured summaries – with key points, to-dos, and next steps. Perfect for sales, support, and management.

### 🎯 Automatically captured:

- **Call topic & context** – What was it about? Who was involved?
- **Action items (to-dos)** – What happens next? With ownership.
- **Next steps & deadlines** – Follow-up meetings, tasks to complete.
- **Decisions** – What was agreed? What commitments were made?
- **Budget, prices, numbers** – All financial details extracted.
- **Contact info & references** – Names, emails, links automatically captured.

## How Does AI Summarization Work?

### 1. Record the call (optional)
CallPlan optionally records the call – or you cite the main points manually.

### 2. AI processes in real-time
During or after the call, CallPlan AI processes the summary – with complete notes.

### 3. Summary in seconds
The AI presents a structured summary with:
- Header & overview
- Action items (bullet points)
- Decisions & agreements
- Next steps with dates

### 4. Send automatically
Done? One click – and the summary goes to your team, boss, or CRM.
