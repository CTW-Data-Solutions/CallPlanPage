## What AI means for calls

With CallPlan AI, manual handoffs are over. Rather than typing notes and searching for next steps, you get structured results automatically – right after the call.

### The intelligent call workflow

- **Call Preparation** – AI gives you context, topics, and the best agenda before the call.
- **Call Summaries** – AI creates a complete recap with action items and decisions.
- **Follow-up** – notes go directly to your team, customer, or CRM.

## How CallPlan AI saves time

- **Less busy work** – instead of 30 minutes of follow-up, get results in seconds

## Best use cases

- **Sales**: prepare leads faster and automatically schedule follow-ups
- **Support**: log customer issues without manual notes
- **Account management**: keep track of customer history and agenda items
- **Management**: document decisions from executive meetings accurately

---

## How it works

1. Create a call in CallPlan.
2. Open AI preparation for context and agenda.
3. Run the call, dictate notes, and let AI create the summary.
4. Send the result automatically to your team or CRM.
