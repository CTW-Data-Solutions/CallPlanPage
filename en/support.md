# Support

## Need help?

We're here for you! If you have a problem or a question about the app, don't hesitate to reach out. Our support team will get back to you as quickly as possible.

### Contact

- **Email support**
  Write to us at [support@quick-id.com](mailto:support@quick-id.com) and we'll respond as soon as possible.

### Frequently Asked Questions

- **How do I reset my password?**
  Visit the [How To](/en/howto/) page and follow the password reset instructions.

- **How do I update my account details?**
  Sign in, go to **Settings**, and update your profile information there.

- **Is there a user guide?**
  Yes – our [How To](/en/howto/) guide covers everything from account creation to advanced features.
