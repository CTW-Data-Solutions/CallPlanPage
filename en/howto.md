# CallPlan User Guide

## Create an Account

1. **Download and install CallPlan**
   Get CallPlan from the App Store.

2. **Create a new account**
   - Open CallPlan on your device.
   - Tap "Create Account".
   - Enter your email address, name, and a secure password.
   - Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox.

3. **Sign in**
   Once verified, sign in with your credentials. You can also use your Apple or Google account for a quick setup.

---

## Configure Your Settings

### 1. Open Settings
- Tap your profile icon in the top-left corner.
- Select "Settings" from the menu.

### 2. Notification preferences
- **Call reminder** – Choose how far in advance you want to be reminded (e.g. 10 or 30 minutes).
- **Automatic note sending** – Choose whether notes are sent automatically after each call: immediately, at end of day, or at a custom time.
- **Recipients** – Add recipients such as your assistant or team members.
- **CarPlay** – Enable or disable CarPlay support for hands-free use while driving.

### 3. Security & Privacy
- **Two-factor authentication** – Add an extra layer of security to your account.
- **Data storage** – Choose between local storage on your device or cloud synchronisation.

## Schedule a Call

1. **Create a new CallPlan**
   - Tap "Create CallPlan".
   - Fill in the details:
     - **Title** – e.g. "Weekly check-in"
     - **Person** – Select a contact from your address book.
     - **Description** – Purpose of the call, e.g. "Discuss project progress".
     - **Date & time** – When should the call take place?
     - **Category** – Business, Internal, or Private.

2. **Set a reminder**
   Choose your lead time (e.g. 15 minutes, 1 hour). Default: 1 day and 1 hour before the call.

3. **Save**
   Tap "Save". You'll receive a notification when the call is due.

---

## Take Notes

1. **After the call**
   Open the completed CallPlan in the app.
   - Add text notes manually.
   - With voice recognition enabled, dictate your thoughts directly.

2. **Audio notes**
   - Tap "Add audio note".
   - Speak into the microphone; tap "Stop" when done.
   - CallPlan transcribes your speech into text, which you can then edit.

3. **Send notes**
   Send notes immediately or at a scheduled time to:
   - Yourself
   - A specific person (e.g. assistant or colleague)
   - Your virtual assistant

## FAQs

### Q1: How do I reset my password?
Tap "Forgot password?" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions in the email you receive.

### Q2: Can I sync CallPlan with Google Calendar?
Yes. Enable Google Calendar sync under Settings.

### Q3: How do I set up Siri shortcuts?
Go to Settings → Siri & Shortcuts and enable the permissions. You can then create custom voice commands to create and manage calls.

### Q4: How do I delete a CallPlan?
Open the CallPlan you want to remove, tap "Delete" at the bottom, and confirm.

### Q5: Does CallPlan store my data?
No. All data remains securely on your device. Cloud sync is only active if you enable it in Settings.

### Q6: What happens if I don't select a category?
Your CallPlan will automatically be assigned the "Unknown" category. You can change it at any time.
