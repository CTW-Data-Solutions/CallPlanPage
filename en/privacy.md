# Privacy Policy

CallPlan requests only the permissions necessary to provide a seamless experience:

- Contacts and Calendars – for managing and scheduling calls
- Voice recognition and microphone access – for audio notes
- Siri integration – for creating call plans via voice commands

**Privacy first!**
CallPlan does not access or store your data. All permissions are used solely for their intended functionality within the app.

**No data sales to advertisers!**
CallPlan does not sell or share data with any advertisers or other third parties.
